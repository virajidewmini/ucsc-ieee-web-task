F.A.Q

  • How do I register for the IEEE Cluster 2022 ?

    Register for the meeting by visiting the IEEE cluster 2022 website. There, you can either complete the online form or print a PDF version of the form and fax or mail it to the UCSC. UCSC accepts all major credit cards, cash, as well as checks for payment.

  • Can I cancel my registration ?

    Requests for refunds will be honoured, less a Rs.500 processing fee, only if received in writing by December 01, 2022. No refunds will be made after December 01, 2022.

  • How can I buy ticket for IEEE Cluster 2022?

    When you complete the IEEE Cluster 2022 registration form, you have the option to purchase tickets on website.If you have problems about the ticket please contact us via ucscieee@gmail.com

  • Can I purchase my tickets when I get to meeting?

    A limited number of tickets for special events may be available at the registration desk, however, we cannot guarantee the availability of tickets on-site.

  • What if I can not attend conference physically, will there be an option to attend virtually?

    Yes, In 2022 we are offering virtual conference for who will not be attend conference physically.Coming soon, we will introduce virtual conference features on our official website.

  • How do I view the schedule?

    You can see the full schedule and sessions at IEEE Cluster 2022 website. please note, schedule is subject to change.
